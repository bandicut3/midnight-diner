About the project

Born in the space between the last track and the walk home.

Midnight Diner is a fashion and music project built on the edges — where texture matters more than polish, and what you wear and what you hear are the same conversation.

Not a brand in the traditional sense. A frequency. Something you tune into when everything else feels like noise.
